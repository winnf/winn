THERMO

We designed a thermoregulatory vest, straying away from the traditional methods such as heating wires/ice pack, and incorporating Arduino boards and Peltier devices (found in CPUs) instead. Applying the unique ability of Peltier devices to generate a hot/cold side using an electric current, it was integrated in the vests to provide a more lightweight, efficient, and safe solution.
